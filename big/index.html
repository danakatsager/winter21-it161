Welcome

Hi and welcome to my BIG webite! This site serves as an example of my abilities in designing modern web sites that are both functional and aesthetically pleasing. If you scroll through the various sections of the menu above, you will find examples of embedded video, calendar functions, image galleries, and maps that are in keeping with modern principles of responsive design.

The explosion of the internet has had unprecedented effects on our sciety and culture, opening up ever more ways for people to interact in realms of society ranging from art to business to simple interpersonal communication. As a web developer, keeping my skill set up to date is vital. There are three ways I go about this.

  • Keeping up with technical developments. Internet technology changes so rapidly, there is no such thing as standing still. There is always another horizon to explore, something new to learn and apply to what may be the greatest communication tool in human history.
  • Keeping up with design trends. Something that goes hand in hand with technological developments are new developments in style and aesthetics. When you have an instantaneous communication medium like the web, what's in and what's out cange rapidly. New ways to present content are vital to add to the user experience.
  • Staying connected to people.At its heart, the web is a means of communication. And that means being able to talk to people, whether it be via text, video, audio, or visual. Despite being largely a visual medium, there are more and more internet users with a disability all the time. And this means taking advantage of ever evolving web standards to ensure that the largest number of people possible are able to access information online in way that is logical and straightforward to them.
